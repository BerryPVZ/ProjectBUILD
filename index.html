Project: BUILD

Free-placement sandbox. Make custom plants, zombies, lawns, bosses, NPCs, props, traps, spawners, projectiles, and collectibles. No grid. Overlap is allowed.

Template Maker

NPCs do not use abilities. They can play a click sound and show multi-slide dialog when clicked in Select mode.

Templates

World

Controls: click to place. Select mode lets you drag, click NPCs for dialog, mouse wheel to scale, Shift + wheel to rotate, Delete to remove selected. GIF assets are displayed as live image overlays so they animate. Combat entities get shadows; props/objects hide health bars.

Click NPC again or press Space for next slide. Press Escape to close.
